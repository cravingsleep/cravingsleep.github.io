About Me

My name is Nick and I'm a software developer for Hotels.com. I'm currently learning to juggle blind and I am using this website to document my progress.

Why?

I've always been interested in how the brain learns and wanted to document a learning process involving something I have enough knowledge in to understand what is going on but also a task challenging enough to take some time in mastering it.
